## Meal Schedule

### Day 1: **Miso Glazed Salmon with Rice & Stir-Fried Vegetables**
**Type:** Home-cooked | **Time:** 35 minutes | **Teen Helper:** Easy veggie prep

**Ingredients:**
- 680g salmon fillets (4 portions)
- 3 tbsp white miso paste
- 2 tbsp mirin
- 2 tbsp soy sauce (low sodium)
- 1 tbsp honey
- Jasmine rice (2 cups dry)
- Broccoli, snap peas, carrots (900g mixed)
- Garlic, ginger, sesame oil

**Instructions:**
1. Marinate salmon in miso, mirin, soy sauce, and honey (15 min)
2. Cook rice according to package
3. Bake salmon at 400°F (200°C) for 12-15 minutes
4. Stir-fry vegetables with garlic, ginger, and sesame oil
5. Serve with rice

**Teen Help:** Chop vegetables, measure rice

### Day 4: **Baked Lemon Herb Chicken Thighs with Orzo & Green Beans**
**Type:** Home-cooked | **Time:** 45 minutes

**Ingredients:**
- 8 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
- 340g orzo pasta
- 450g green beans, trimmed
- 2 lemons (zested and juiced)
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp fresh herbs (thyme, rosemary, parsley)
- 3 cups chicken broth
- Olive oil
- Salt, pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
2. Season chicken thighs with lemon zest, herbs, salt, pepper
3. Sear chicken skin-side down in oven-safe skillet, 5 minutes
4. Flip and transfer skillet to oven, bake 25-30 minutes
5. Meanwhile, cook orzo in chicken broth
6. Blanch or sauté green beans with garlic
7. Serve chicken over orzo with green beans, drizzle with lemon juice

**Teen Help:** Trim green beans, zest lemons, measure ingredients

### Day 7: **One-Pot Creamy Tuscan Pasta with Chicken**
**Type:** Home-cooked | **Time:** 30 minutes

**Ingredients:**
- 450g chicken thighs or breast, cubed
- 450g penne pasta
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 can diced tomatoes
- 3 cups fresh spinach
- 4 garlic cloves
- Sun-dried tomatoes (optional)
- Parmesan cheese
- Italian herbs

**Instructions:**
1. Brown chicken in large pot
2. Add garlic, cook 1 minute
3. Add pasta, broth, tomatoes; bring to boil
4. Simmer 12-15 minutes until pasta tender
5. Stir in cream, spinach, and cheese
6. Serve with parmesan

**Teen Help:** Cube chicken, measure ingredients, stir

### Day 8: **Shrimp Fried Rice with Mixed Vegetables**
**Type:** Home-cooked | **Time:** 25 minutes

**Ingredients:**
- 450g shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 4 cups cooked rice (day-old rice works best)
- 3 eggs, beaten
- 2 cups mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn, edamame)
- 4 scallions, chopped
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- White pepper, salt

**Instructions:**
1. Heat wok or large skillet over high heat
2. Scramble eggs, set aside
3. Stir-fry shrimp until pink, 2-3 minutes, set aside
4. Add vegetables, stir-fry 3 minutes
5. Add rice, break up clumps, stir-fry 3-4 minutes
6. Add garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil
7. Return shrimp and eggs, toss everything together
8. Garnish with scallions

**Teen Help:** Prep vegetables, beat eggs, measure sauces

---

### Day 9: **Asian Black Cod with Bok Choy & Rice Noodles**
**Type:** Home-cooked | **Time:** 30 minutes

**Ingredients:**
- 680g black cod fillets
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp honey
- 4-5 heads baby bok choy
- Rice noodles (340g)
- Scallions, sesame seeds
- Garlic, ginger

**Instructions:**
1. Marinate cod in soy, vinegar, sesame oil, honey (10 min)
2. Cook rice noodles according to package
3. Pan-sear cod 3-4 min per side (or bake at 400°F/200°C for 12 min)
4. Sauté bok choy with garlic and ginger
5. Serve cod over noodles with bok choy

**Teen Help:** Prep bok choy, cook noodles, make marinade

## Prep Tips

### Make-Ahead Suggestions

- Miso salmon (Day 1) - can freeze raw marinated fillets
- Lemon herb chicken (Day 4) - can freeze raw marinated chicken thighs

### Time-Savers
- Buy pre-riced cauliflower
- Use rotisserie chicken for Day 7 if short on time
- Pre-cut vegetables available in produce section
