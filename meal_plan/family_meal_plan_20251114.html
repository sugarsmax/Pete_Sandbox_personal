Day 1: Miso Glazed Salmon with Rice & Stir-Fried Vegetables
Home-cooked | Time: 35 minutes | Teen Helper: Easy veggie prep
Ingredients:
  • 680g salmon fillets (4 portions)
  • 3 tbsp white miso paste
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce (low sodium)
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Jasmine rice (2 cups dry)
  • Broccoli, snap peas, carrots (900g mixed)
  • Garlic, ginger, sesame oil
Instructions:
  1. Marinate salmon in miso, mirin, soy sauce, and honey (15 min)
  2. Cook rice according to package
  3. Bake salmon at 400°F (200°C) for 12-15 minutes
  4. Stir-fry vegetables with garlic, ginger, and sesame oil
  5. Serve with rice
Teen Help: Chop vegetables, measure rice

Day 3: Black Bean & Sweet Potato Burrito Bowls
Home-cooked | Time: 30 minutes | Uses: Mexican leftovers | Leftovers: Yes
Ingredients:
  • Leftover rice and beans from Day 2
  • 2 large sweet potatoes, cubed
  • 1 can black beans (additional)
  • Bell peppers (3, various colors)
  • Corn (fresh or frozen, 350g)
  • Avocado, lime, cilantro
  • Sour cream, cheese, lettuce
  • Cumin, paprika, garlic powder
Instructions:
  1. Roast sweet potato cubes at 425°F (220°C) for 25 minutes
  2. Sauté peppers and corn with spices
  3. Warm leftover rice and beans
  4. Assemble bowls with all toppings
Make Extra: Double the sweet potatoes and peppers for Day 5
Teen Help: Cube sweet potatoes, chop vegetables, assemble bowls
Day 4: Baked Lemon Herb Chicken Thighs with Orzo & Green Beans
Home-cooked | Time: 45 minutes
Ingredients:
  • 8 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 340g orzo pasta
  • 450g green beans, trimmed
  • 2 lemons (zested and juiced)
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh herbs (thyme, rosemary, parsley)
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
  2. Season chicken thighs with lemon zest, herbs, salt, pepper
  3. Sear chicken skin-side down in oven-safe skillet, 5 minutes
  4. Flip and transfer skillet to oven, bake 25-30 minutes
  5. Meanwhile, cook orzo in chicken broth
  6. Blanch or sauté green beans with garlic
  7. Serve chicken over orzo with green beans, drizzle with lemon juice
Teen Help: Trim green beans, zest lemons, measure ingredients
Day 5: Sheet Pan Chicken Fajitas with Cauliflower Rice
Home-cooked | Time: 35 minutes
Ingredients:
  • 900g chicken breast, sliced
  • Leftover peppers from Day 3
  • 2 onions, sliced

  4. Serve with tortillas and toppings
Teen Help: Slice chicken and onions, prep toppings bar

Day 7: Crispy Teriyaki Tofu with Broccoli & Rice
Home-cooked | Time: 35 minutes
Ingredients:
  • 400g extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • Jasmine rice (2 cups dry)
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce (low sodium)
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • Fresh ginger, grated
  • Sesame seeds
  • Scallions for garnish
  • Vegetable oil for frying
Instructions:
  1. Press tofu to remove excess water, cut into 1-inch cubes
  2. Cook rice according to package
  3. Mix soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, sesame oil, cornstarch for teriyaki sauce
  4. Pan-fry tofu in vegetable oil until golden and crispy on all sides (8-10 min)
  5. Steam or stir-fry broccoli with garlic and ginger
  6. Add teriyaki sauce to tofu, toss until coated and sauce thickens
  7. Serve tofu and broccoli over rice, garnish with sesame seeds and scallions
Teen Help: Press tofu, cube tofu, measure sauce ingredients, cook rice
Day 8: Shrimp Fried Rice with Mixed Vegetables
Home-cooked | Time: 25 minutes
Ingredients:
  • 450g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cups cooked rice (day-old rice works best)
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn, edamame)
  • 4 scallions, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • White pepper, salt
Instructions:
  1. Heat wok or large skillet over high heat
  2. Scramble eggs, set aside
  3. Stir-fry shrimp until pink, 2-3 minutes, set aside
  4. Add vegetables, stir-fry 3 minutes
  5. Add rice, break up clumps, stir-fry 3-4 minutes
  6. Add garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil
  7. Return shrimp and eggs, toss everything together
  8. Garnish with scallions
Teen Help: Prep vegetables, beat eggs, measure sauces
Day 9: Asian Black Cod with Bok Choy & Rice Noodles
Home-cooked | Time: 30 minutes
Ingredients:
  • 680g black cod fillets
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 4-5 heads baby bok choy
  • Rice noodles (340g)
  • Scallions, sesame seeds
  • Garlic, ginger
Instructions:
  1. Marinate cod in soy, vinegar, sesame oil, honey (10 min)
  2. Cook rice noodles according to package
  3. Pan-sear cod 3-4 min per side (or bake at 400°F/200°C for 12 min)
  4. Sauté bok choy with garlic and ginger
  5. Serve cod over noodles with bok choy
Teen Help: Prep bok choy, cook noodles, make marinade
